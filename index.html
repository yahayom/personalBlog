Jebel Aakdar

Jebel Aakdar is considered one of the most important tourist places in the Sultanate of Oman. Jebel Aakdar is characterized by a mild climate throughout the year, and a cold winter.Jebel Aakdar is about 2980m above sea level, and about 139km away from Muscat Governorate. Note: four-wheel drive vehicle must be used to climb the mountain.

Wadi Bani Khalid

The Wilayat of Wadi Bani Khalid is located in Al Sharqiyah North Governorate. Wadi Bani Khalid is characterized by the surrounding mountains and palm trees. Many activities can be done, such as swimming, hiking and other activities. Wadi Bani Khalid is about 233 km away from Muscat.

Bidiyah

Bidiyah is located in Al Sharqiyah North Governorate. In the golden sands, many activities can be done, such as sand boarding, camping and other activities. Bidiyah is about 186 km away from Muscat Governorate.
