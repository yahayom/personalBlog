Jebel Aakdar

Jebel Aakdar is considered one of the most important tourist places in the Sultanate of Oman. Jebel Aakdar is characterized by a mild climate throughout the year, and a cold winter.Jebel Aakdar is about 2980m above sea level, and about 139km away from Muscat Governorate. Note: A four-wheel drive vehicle must be used to climb the mountain.
